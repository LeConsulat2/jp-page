## Personal Positioning

Jonathan Park should not be described primarily as a data analyst.

He is a product-minded, operations-aware, design-conscious technical generalist. His value comes from combining business understanding, workflow thinking, communication clarity, visual sensibility, and practical technical execution.

He understands products and services deeply, sees the full system, identifies friction quickly, and improves both the internal process and the external presentation. His skill set branches across operations, service design, reporting, Excel, Python, SQL, automation, dashboards, digital content, and front-end or visual thinking.

His brand should feel:

- versatile
- sharp
- thoughtful
- practical
- modern
- visually aware
- technically capable
- business-facing
- not boxed into a single specialist label

Avoid framing him as:

- only a data analyst
- purely technical
- only a developer
- only an operations person

Prefer framing him as:

- all-rounder
- product-minded problem solver
- cross-functional builder
- operations + design + technical generalist
- someone who bridges structure, systems, insight, and presentation
